## 🌐 How pulsecast Works

pulsecast manages digital signs by syncing content in real time using SSE (Server-Sent Events). This means changes you make on your PC reach connected screens immediately when online. It also keeps a backup of content on each screen. If the internet goes down, screens keep showing saved content without interruption.

The app lets you assign roles and permissions. That means you can allow some people to only view reports, while others can upload and change content.

## 🗂️ Managing Your Digital Signs

Here are some key actions you can perform:

- **Add new screens**: Connect new signage devices to pulsecast by entering their unique IDs.

- **Upload content**: Add images, videos, or playlists directly through the interface.

- **Schedule playlists**: Choose when certain content should appear, like promotions during business hours.

- **Check screen status**: See if signs are online or offline and what content they currently show.

- **Use offline caching**: Screens will keep playing content even if they lose connection.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **Pulsecast won’t open**: Restart your computer. Check if your antivirus blocked the app.

- **Content not showing on screens**: Ensure screens are powered on and connected to the network.

- **Updates delay**: Verify your internet connection and restart pulsecast.

- **Lost login credentials**: Contact your system administrator or reset your password on the login screen if available.
